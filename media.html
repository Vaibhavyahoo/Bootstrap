Steve Smith Austrilia

Steven Smith started out mainly as a leg-spinner, before he established himself as the premier batsman of Australia. He plays for the New South Wales Blues domestically in Australia. Having shown outstanding promise during his early years, Smith made his international debut in an ODI against the West Indies in 2010.

Virat Kohali India

Virat Kohli born 5 November 1988) is an Indian international cricketer who currently captains the Indian cricket team. A right-handed batsman often regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world, Kohli was ranked eighth in ESPN's list of world's most famous athletes in 2016

Joe Root England

Joseph Edward "Joe" Root (born 30 December 1990) is an English cricketer, who plays for Yorkshire County Cricket Club and England. He was originally a right-handed opening batsman and occasional off-spinner, with a similar playing style to former England captain Michael Vaughan. However, he has played the majority of his cricket for England in the middle order.

Kane Stuart Williamson (born 8 August 1990) is a professional cricketer and currently the captain of New Zealand. He is a right-handestyle batsman and occasional off-spinner. As of 20 December 2016, he is the fourth most highly ranked batsman in Test cricket. Domestically he has represented Northern Districts in New Zealand domestic cricket, the Sunrisers Hyderabad in the IPL.
